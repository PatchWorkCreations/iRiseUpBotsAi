Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is iRiseUp.ai?

iRiseUp.ai is your all-in-one team of 11 AI-powered assistants—ready to help you with business, marketing, productivity, wellness, education, emotional support, and more. It’s like hiring experts—without the salaries or stress.

❓ Do I need technical skills to use this?

Nope! Everything is ready for you. If you can chat, you can use iRiseUp.ai. No setup. No coding. Just smart conversations that actually help.

❓ Is there a free trial?

Yes! All you have to do is sign up—no credit card required. You’ll get access to Lumos (Emotional Support AI), 8 chats per day, and a risk-free taste of what iRiseUp can do.

❓ How many AI assistants do I get?

Even on the free plan, you get full access to all 11 AI assistants—no upgrades required. Each assistant is designed for a specific task like business, marketing, education, or wellness, so you always get the right help at the right time.

❓ Can I create my own AI assistant?

Yes! With the Pro Plan, you can build, customize, and train your very own assistant using your own data. Whether it's client docs, internal SOPs, or FAQs—your AI learns it all.
