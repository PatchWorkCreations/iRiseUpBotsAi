Privacy Policy

This policy explains how iRiseUp.ai collects, uses, shares, and protects your information across our website, apps, and AI services.

Effective date: May 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to deliver and improve your experience.

  • Account data: name, email address, password hash, and plan details.
  • Usage data: product interactions, device/browser data, and performance diagnostics.
  • Conversation data: prompts and responses used to provide AI features and support.
  • Payment data: processed by secure third-party providers; we do not store full card numbers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate accounts, subscriptions, and core product functionality.
  • To personalize your experience and improve response quality.
  • To detect abuse, enforce security controls, and prevent fraud.
  • To communicate critical service, billing, or policy updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with vetted service providers for hosting, analytics, support, and payment processing under contractual confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for legal, operational, and service requirements. We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including access controls, monitoring, and encrypted transport where applicable.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete eligible account information.
  • Manage communication preferences from account settings.
  • Request support for data rights at support@iriseup.ai.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
