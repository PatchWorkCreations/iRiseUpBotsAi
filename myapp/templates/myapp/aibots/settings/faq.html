FAQs

iRiseUp.AI FAQ

Where did all my brilliant conversations go?

Don’t worry, your genius hasn’t vanished into the AI abyss! Just glide your eyes to the top-right corner of your screen and click “History.” There, you’ll find all your past chats lined up like greatest hits.

Want to find that one convo where you dropped a truth bomb? Use the search bar like a digital detective.

How do I upgrade to iRiseUp Pro?

Already using iRiseUp.AI? Awesome. Just log in with your usual method. Then click your name in the top-right corner and select “Upgrade your plan.”

Using the iOS app? If the subscribe button is grayed-out, it means Apple is still activating the feature. Hang tight!

What is iRiseUp Pro?

iRiseUp Pro is your AI fast pass—faster responses, access to new features, and smoother performance during peak hours.

Is the free plan still a thing?

Yes! You can still use iRiseUp for free, even without logging in. Our Pro plan helps fund the platform so everyone can enjoy access.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Click your profile icon → "My Plan" → "Manage my subscription." Then click “Cancel Plan” on the Stripe page. Your plan stays active until the billing cycle ends.

Note: Cancel at least 24 hours before the next billing to avoid charges. Fees are non-refundable unless required by law.

My account got terminated. Can I get a refund?

If your account violated our Terms of Use, refunds aren’t provided. You’ll still be responsible for any pending fees.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit and debit cards. No pre-paid, gift cards, or checks—sorry, Grandma.

Are there usage limits?

Yes, each plan has a quota. Pro users may have dynamic limits during busy times to keep the platform fair for all.

Is there an annual plan?

Yes! Pay $200 upfront for a full year of iRiseUp Pro—one payment, 365 days of awesome.

How much does it cost to use iRiseUp?

It’s free! But Pro unlocks the VIP perks. See our Pricing Page for full details.

How does iRiseUp AI work?

We use a fine-tuned GPT-3.5 model optimized through RLHF (that’s fancy AI training with human help). It’s like your genius friend—but digital.

Why does the AI sound so real?

Because it’s trained on tons of human-written content. It’s good at sounding like your smart, slightly sassy best friend.

Can I trust the AI’s answers?

Sometimes. It’s not connected to the internet and its knowledge ends around 2021. It’s smart—but not psychic.

Who can see my conversations?

We may review chats to improve safety and performance—but we don’t display or sell your data. Privacy matters.

Can I delete specific prompts?

Not yet. So avoid anything too personal, secret, or related to royal family affairs.

Can I see my past chats?

Yep! Click the “History” icon in the top-right. Pro tip: Save gold conversations elsewhere too—just in case.

Why did the AI give a weird answer?

It may have “hallucinated”—a real term for when AI gets creative in a bad way. Email us at hello@iriseup.ai if it gets too weird.
