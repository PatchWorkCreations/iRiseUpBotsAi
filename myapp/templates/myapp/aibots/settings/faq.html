FAQs

General

What is iRiseUp.AI?

iRiseUp.AI is an advanced AI platform designed to provide expert guidance in business, wellness, creativity, and personal development. Unlike traditional AI chatbots, it offers a suite of specialized AI assistants to support various aspects of your journey.

How do I use iRiseUp.AI?

Getting started is simple! Sign up for an account to unlock access to multiple AI assistants. Each assistant is designed to help with specific needs, such as business strategy, wellness, marketing, or financial guidance. Choose the one that fits your goals and start your conversation.

Is iRiseUp.AI free to use?

Yes! iRiseUp.AI offers free access so users can explore its features. However, to provide the best experience for those who rely on it for deeper engagement, guest access is limited. A premium membership with enhanced features is also in development.

Do I need an account to use iRiseUp.AI?

Guests can explore iRiseUp.AI with limited access to Lumos and select AI assistants. However, creating an account unlocks access to all assistants, saves chat history, and provides a more personalized experience.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the settings panel under "Account Management." Please note that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be reversed.

What AI assistants are available?

iRiseUp.AI features a diverse range of AI assistants, each with a unique role:

  • Elevate – A motivator for business insights and personal growth.

  • Mentor IQ – A learning and career advisor providing accessible knowledge.
  • Nexara – A marketing strategist for branding, digital growth, and business expansion.
  • Keystone – A finance and legal guide simplifying budgeting and business structuring.
  • Pathfinder – A wisdom-based AI offering insights into faith, knowledge, and life’s deeper questions.
